Creating with clay

Creation!

  • Bend and fold clay into blob to soften it
  • Squeeze, roll, and poke into the desired shape
  • Use other colors in places you'd like for detail
  • Take the desired shape and use tools to make detailing marks

Baking!

  1. Preheat oven to the package instructions. (varies on different clays)
  2. Use oven safe sheet to bake on. clay may stain
  3. Bake for the allotted time on the packaging.
  4. for larger pieces use wires to support them.
  5. when finished baking remove from oven and let it cool.
