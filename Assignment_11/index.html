The carnivorous cowplant

The Cowplant is a plant within the sims universe. A very strange plant that is found only a few ways, such as foraging for it after a certain skill level or through space travel when getting that job level. The cow plant grows from a seed that grows into a tiny plant with horns (see right), Personally I find the plant cute in all stages.

To get the Sim to be able to get the ability to ge the plant is through work progress as an astronaut or as a gardener. Their work in either one will result in the possibilty of getting the plant seed, along with other things that can help your sim or simple collectables.

The fully grown cowplant as seen on the left is a huge plant with a cake as a tongue. They can be played with, fed, or ignored if so choosen to. However with being ignored they will use that tongue as bait. They will lure sims in with the cake and can eat sims if they are willing to try grabbing it without intervention.
